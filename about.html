Founding Story

UbuntuSave was founded to support street kids in Rwanda by providing shelter, education, and mentorship. Our team is passionate about creating lasting change through community-driven initiatives.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower street children through care, education, and community support. We envision a future where every child has the opportunity to thrive and contribute to society.
