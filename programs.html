Our Programs

UbuntuSave offers a range of programs designed to support street kids in Rwanda, focusing on shelter, education, health, mentorship, and reintegration.

Shelter

Safe and secure housing offered in multiple locations, supporting over 500 kids. Eligibility is based on need and vulnerability assessments.

Education

Subjects taught include literacy, numeracy, and life skills. We partner with local schools and provide digital learning access.

Health & Nutrition

Daily meals are provided, along with regular medical checkups and mental health support.

Mentorship & Counseling

Volunteer stories and youth life coaching help children build confidence and resilience.

Reintegration & Life Skills

Programs to help kids transition to independence, including vocational training and community reintegration.
